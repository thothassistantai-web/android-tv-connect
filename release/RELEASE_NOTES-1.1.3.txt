Phase 1 seamless connection UX (v1.1.3).

- Neutral ADB defaults: auto-discover first USB / wireless device; legacy dev serials migrate on load.
- Header chips show capture node and ADB serial with transport; ADB chip refreshes and reconnects.
- Hot-plug toast when configured USB serial vanishes and one new device appears (Switch / Dismiss).
- Mismatch banner when capture is active, multiple ADB devices visible, and wired serial is not auto.
- Settings labels: Auto (first USB), Auto (first wireless); note that capture and ADB are independent.
- Strategy doc: docs/SEAMLESS-UX.md
